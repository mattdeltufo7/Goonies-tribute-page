The Goonies

The Goonies Movie Poster

"The Goonies" is a classic 1985 adventure film directed by Richard Donner. The story follows a group of kids from Astoria, as they embark on a treasure hunt to save their homes from foreclosure.

The movie has become a beloved cult classic and is known for its memorable characters, thrilling adventure, and enduring sense of nostalgia.
